GRADE 4

Level 1: BASIC FUNCTIONS OF THE COMPUTER OPERATIONS

MECHANICS & RULES

Players are given only 12 seconds to answer each "4 Pics 1 Word" related to the topic "BASIC FUNCTIONS OF THE COMPUTER OPERATIONS". They must analyze the images and quickly deduce the word that connects them.

The objective is to correctly guess the word that relates to the four images provided. Each player starts with 3 lives. A life is lost for each incorrect guess or if the time limit elapses without providing an answer. Once all lives are lost, the game ends.
